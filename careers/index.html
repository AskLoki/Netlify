Come do the most meaningful work of your career

At Facebook, our mission is to give people the power to build community and bring the world closer together.

When we say design, we mean it comprehensively.

Our multidisciplinary team approach involves product designers, content strategists, user experience researchers, communication designers, industrial designers, sound designers, creative operations specialists and more. Our collaboration with engineering and other cross-functional partners places design at the core of how we address product challenges.

We value empathy, quality and intentionality. We hire people who are talented, self-aware and proactive. By fostering a strengths-based environment, we set people up to do the best work of their careers.

Why join our design community?

Designing for billions of people is one of the most complex design tasks any team can tackle—and no designer can do it alone. Together, Facebook design teams create, build and shape communication experiences in service of the essential, universal human need for connection. It's never been more important to incorporate a diverse range of perspectives to reflect the diversity of people who use our products.

If you want to be part of solving the most complex design problems for the greatest number of people, join us!

Apply to open roles across our global design community in Menlo Park, San Francisco, Seattle, New York, London, Tel Aviv, and elsewhere.



Some Of Our Roles


Product Designers ensure our products and features are valuable for people, easy to use, and of the highest level of craft and execution.

Product Design Managers lead and mentor teams to guide them to success and advocate for design across the company.

Content Strategists give our products a voice and maintain content systems that ensure our products have consistency, clarity and compassion.

UX Researchers validate real needs through listening to people and unlock insights that guide our work.

Design Program Managers connect teams and solve problems to enable effective collaboration.

Sound Designers create unique, memorable, and landmark sonic experiences for billions of people.

Opportunities for Students and Grads


The University Grad Program helps recent graduates transition from university to a career in technology and design. You'll hit the ground running, learn from the best in the industry and build products for billions of people around the world. Learn more

The Internship Program at Facebook is the ultimate learning experience. World-class mentors, our open culture and opportunities to make a real impact are just a few reasons why our intern program was ranked #1 by Glassdoor two years in a row. Learn more

Facebook University is a hands-on, immersive internship program that enables students from underrepresented communities to get to know Facebook’s people, products and services. Learn more

Campus and Office Events are hosted on a rolling basis and cover a range of topics from interview preparation and personal portfolio reviews to targeted design workshops. Be on the lookout for these opportunities from our University Recruiting team and on our social media pages.
